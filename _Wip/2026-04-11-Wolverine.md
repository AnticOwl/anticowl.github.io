What Remains of Edith Finch is a collection of strange tales about a family in Washington state. As Edith, you’ll explore the colossal Finch house, searching for stories as she explores her family history and tries to figure out why she's the last one in her family left alive.

## Required
* [X-Men Origins: Wolverine Cheat Table](https://anticowl.github.io/files/wolverine/wolverine.ct)
* [HUD and Pause Menu Toggler](https://anticowl.github.io/files/wolverine/ShaderToggler.ini)
* [Cheat Engine 7.6 or higher](https://du0wcodktyky8.cloudfront.net/installer/003333/420362877561915)
* [ReShade with addon support](https://reshade.me)
* [Shader Toggler x86](https://github.com/FransBouma/ShaderToggler/releases/tag/1.2.1)

## Configuration

Install ReShade and Shader Toggler addon in `\Medal of Honor\Binaries`. Do not forget to also copy previously downloaded ini at the same place.  
It will be used to remove the pause menu while in pause.

## How To
### Use The Cheat Table
Once you have installed Cheat Engine 7.6 or higher (if not already done), click twice on the cheat table file you downloaded. Check the `Auto Attach to Game` box. Cheat Engine will automatically connect to the game when detected.
Press `Page Down` to enable the camera structure and leave on.

### Take A Screenshot
* Press `Escape` on keyboard or `Pause` on your controller to pause the game.
* Press `Delete` to disable the pause menu
* Frame, take your shot and go the other way around to get back in game.
